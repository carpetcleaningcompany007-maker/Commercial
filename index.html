The experience

A refined coffee stop that feels part of the day, not bolted on.

Coffee at Margot's suits hosts who want something warmer than a standard catering table and more memorable than a drinks urn in the corner. The horsebox gives guests somewhere to gather, talk, choose a cake and enjoy a proper coffee while the event keeps its sense of occasion.

It can be styled for relaxed garden parties, smart country weddings, private family celebrations, business open days and seasonal events around Ludlow and the surrounding villages.

Coffee and cakes

A chalkboard menu with character, proper coffee and generous cake-counter energy.

The menu can be shaped to suit the booking: espresso, flat whites, lattes, hot chocolate, chai, iced coffee and a cake selection that feels inviting from the moment guests walk up.

  • Espresso, Americano, flat white, latte, cappuccino and mocha
  • Hot chocolate, chai, iced coffees and seasonal options
  • Victoria sponge, chocolate cake, carrot cake, lemon drizzle and treats

Presentation

Smart enough for a wedding. Warm enough for a village green.

Front service. Coffee and hot drinks served from the front of the horsebox, giving guests a clear focal point.

Side display. Cakes and treats presented from the side hatch so the setup feels abundant and inviting.

Country-luxury feel. A dark chalkboard palette, warm timber tones and handwritten-style details create a memorable look without feeling fussy.
