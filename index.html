How the process works

We choose the method based on the carpet type, soil level, drying requirements, and how the building is used.

  • Survey and plan
    Access, timing, high traffic areas and risk points
  • Pre spray and agitation
    Lifts soil so it can be removed, not masked
  • Extraction or low moisture clean
    Chosen for the site to balance results and drying time
  • Walkthrough and handover
    Focus areas checked, advice given, next visit scheduled
