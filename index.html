FibreLife is a maintenance system, not a one-off clean

FibreLife combines controlled chemistry, mechanical agitation and encapsulation to remove embedded soil while avoiding over-wetting, wick-back and long drying times.

This allows commercial carpets to be cleaned more frequently, stay cleaner for longer, and deliver measurable asset life extension.
