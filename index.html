The idea

A coffee stop that looks like it belongs at the event.

Coffee at Margot's is for hosts who want something more memorable than a catering table. The horsebox becomes a small destination at the event: guests can gather, order a proper coffee, choose cake and drift back into the day with something worth holding.

The style is relaxed but polished, making it a strong fit for country weddings, garden parties, open days, local shows and celebrations around Ludlow.
