FibreLife is not a clean — it is a system

A structured maintenance approach designed to protect commercial carpet assets.

FibreLife combines controlled chemistry, mechanical agitation and encapsulation technology to remove embedded soil while avoiding over-wetting, wick-back and extended drying times.

This allows carpets to be maintained more frequently, preserving appearance, improving hygiene and significantly extending carpet lifespan across commercial environments.
