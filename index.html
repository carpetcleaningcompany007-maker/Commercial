what is FibreLife Technology

FibreLife is a commercial carpet maintenance system that combines controlled chemistry, mechanical agitation and encapsulation to remove embedded soil without overwetting.

It is designed specifically for environments where traditional hot water extraction causes disruption, long drying times and premature wear.

the FibreLife commercial process

  1. Assess traffic patterns, fibre type and usage.
  2. Apply calibrated FibreLife solution.

  4. Dry quickly and return the area to service.
