FibreLife Technology

A professional carpet maintenance system engineered for commercial environments where appearance, uptime and longevity matter.

FibreLife is a controlled deep-cleaning process that combines specialist chemistry, mechanical agitation and encapsulation technology. It removes embedded soil while minimising downtime, overwetting and disruption.

Designed for offices, hotels, conference centres and large public spaces where traditional extraction is impractical.

The FibreLife commercial process

  1. Assessment of traffic patterns, fibre type and soiling.
  2. Application of FibreLife solution calibrated for the environment.

  4. Controlled drying allowing rapid return to service.
  5. Ongoing maintenance planning to extend carpet lifespan.
