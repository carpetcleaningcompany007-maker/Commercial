FibreLife Technology

A commercial carpet maintenance system designed to outperform traditional extraction.

FibreLife combines controlled chemistry, mechanical agitation and encapsulation to remove embedded soil while avoiding overwetting and extended drying times.

The result is consistent appearance standards, reduced disruption and extended carpet lifespan across commercial environments.

commercial focus

Large commercial premises

The FibreLife commercial process

  1. Site assessment of traffic, fibre type and usage.
  2. Calibrated application of FibreLife solution.
  3. Mechanical agitation to lift and encapsulate soil.
  4. Rapid drying enabling immediate return to service.
  5. Maintenance planning to protect long-term investment.
